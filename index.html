About Us

We Provide Quality CBC Junior School Education

Kathaayoni Academy Junior School is a mixed-day and boarding school offering Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC) education for Grades 7 to 9. Located in the heart of Nairobi, we are committed to nurturing academically excellent, morally upright, and socially responsible young people.

Our school blends rigorous academics with sports, arts, ICT, spiritual formation, and counseling to ensure every learner reaches their full potential.

📚
CBC-Aligned Curriculum Fully aligned with Kenya's Competency-Based Curriculum for Grades 7–9
🏆
Award-Winning Programme Consistently recognised for academic and co-curricular excellence
🌿
Holistic Development Sports, arts, ICT, counseling and spiritual formation for every student
