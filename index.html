About Kathaayoni Academy

School History

Founded in 1985, Kathaayoni Academy has been a beacon of educational excellence for over three decades. We started with a vision to provide quality education that nurtures both academic achievement and character development.

Location

Located in the heart of the city, our campus spans 15 acres of beautifully landscaped grounds, providing an ideal environment for learning and growth.
