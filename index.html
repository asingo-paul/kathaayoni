About Us

About Kathaayoni Academy

Kathaayoni Academy is a nurturing and progressive learning institution committed to providing quality education that supports the holistic development of every learner. Since its establishment in 2017, the school has grown steadily into a respected learning center offering the Competency-Based Education (CBE) curriculum.

Located in a calm and secure environment in Machakos, we provide a safe and welcoming space where learners can thrive academically, socially, and morally. Our school community is built on the values of discipline, respect, responsibility, and excellence.

Complete Learning Pathway Playgroup, PP1, PP2, Primary (Grade 1–6) and Junior School (Grade 7–9)
Modern Facilities Science laboratory and computer lab for practical and digital learning
Boarding Facilities A safe, supportive home away from home for learners to grow in character and independence

Boarding Life

Boarding Life at Kathaayoni Academy

A safe, nurturing home away from home where learners grow academically, socially, and personally.

Our Boarding Philosophy

At Kathaayoni Academy, boarding is not simply accommodation for learners — it is a supportive community where students live, learn, and grow together. We strive to create a warm environment where every learner feels safe, cared for, and encouraged to develop academically, socially, and emotionally.

Why Choose Boarding at Kathaayoni Academy?

Safe and Secure Environment

Learners are under the care of responsible house parents and trained boarding staff who provide supervision and guidance throughout the day and night.

A Caring, Home-Like Atmosphere

We aim to create a welcoming environment where every learner feels valued, supported, and comfortable.

Holistic Development

Boarders participate in academics, sports, music, arts, leadership activities, and other programs that develop their talents and confidence.

Guided Study Sessions

Evening prep sessions are organized to support learning, reinforce classroom work, and help learners develop strong study habits.

Healthy and Balanced Meals

Learners are provided with nutritious meals that support their health, concentration, and overall wellbeing.

Weekend Activities

Engaging weekend programs allow learners to relax, socialize, and explore new interests in a fun and structured way.

FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

1. What curriculum does Kathaayoni Academy offer?

Kathaayoni Academy follows the Kenyan Competency-Based Education (CBE) curriculum, offering learning from kindergarten to Grade 9.

2. Which levels are available at the school?

The school provides a full learning journey starting from Playgroup, PP1, PP2, Primary (Grade 1–6), and Junior School (Grade 7–9).

3. Does the school promote Christian values?

Yes. Kathaayoni Academy is guided by strong Christian values, which help nurture discipline, integrity, and respect among learners.

4. What co-curricular activities are offered?

Learners participate in a variety of activities such as sports, music, arts, STEM programs, clubs, leadership development, and community service.

5. How is the academic performance of the school?

Kathaayoni Academy has consistently demonstrated strong academic performance in the CBE system, with learners progressing to reputable schools.

6. Does the school provide transport services?

Yes. The school offers safe and reliable transport within Machakos and nearby areas.
