About the Junior Secondary Assessment (JSA)

At the end of Grade 9, learners sit the Junior Secondary Assessment (JSA), a national assessment administered by the Kenya National Examinations Council (KNEC). Performance in the JSA determines placement into Senior Secondary Schools (Grades 10–12) and the pathway selected (Science & Technology, Arts & Sports Science, or Social Sciences).
