Supporting Every Learner's Journey

The Guidance and Counseling department at Kathaayoni Academy is vital for promoting the holistic development and well-being of students. It provides valuable support in academic, personal, emotional, and career-related areas, helping students overcome challenges and reach their full potential.

Our team of trained counselors offers both individual and group counseling sessions, career guidance, and life skills training to prepare students for life beyond school.

Mental Health SupportConfidential counseling to help students manage stress, anxiety, and personal challenges
Career GuidanceCareer talks, aptitude assessments and pathway planning for post-junior secondary
Parent EngagementRegular parent-counselor meetings to support student development collaboratively
