Spiritual Formation & Character Development

Faith and Religion at Kathaayoni Academy is fundamentally concerned with the spiritual development of students. It aims to nurture their faith, deepen their relationship with God, and help them grow spiritually.

Our faith programme focuses on virtues such as humility, integrity, kindness, and service to others. We celebrate religious diversity while anchoring our community in shared values of love, respect, and responsibility.
