Agriculture & Nutrition Education

Agriculture education equips our pupils with practical skills, encourages a sense of responsibility, and provides valuable insights into the food system, the environment, and the global economy. It prepares students for careers in the agricultural sector.

Our school farm serves as a living classroom where students grow crops, rear small animals, and learn about sustainable farming practices. The farm produces fresh vegetables used in the school kitchen.
