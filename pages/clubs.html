Clubs & Societies

Clubs and societies offer students a diverse range of experiences for personal and academic growth, complementing formal education and building social and professional networks.

Technology

ICT & Coding Club

Students build websites, create apps, and solve technology problems in our popular coding club. Participation in national hackathons is encouraged.

STEM

Science Club

Hands-on experiments, science fairs, and guest talks from scientists and innovators bring science to life outside the classroom.

Leadership

Debate & Public Speaking

Students develop confidence, articulation and critical thinking through competitive debates and public speaking events at school and regional level.

Arts

Music & Drama Club

Choir, instrumental music, dance and drama productions give students a creative outlet and develop performance and presentation skills.

Faith

Christian Union

A platform for fellowship, worship, Bible study, and community service projects that help students grow in faith and character.

Environment

Environment & Agriculture Club

Tree planting, recycling, school garden management and environmental awareness campaigns led by passionate student environmentalists.
