Junior Secondary School – Grades 7 to 9

The Junior School at Kathaayoni Academy is the lower secondary level of education, encompassing Grades 7, 8, and 9 (students aged approximately 12 to 15 years). This level is aligned with Kenya's Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC).

Junior secondary education aims to provide students with a solid foundation in core subjects while nurturing their abilities through practical learning, projects, and self-expression activities.

Core subjects include English, Mathematics, Science and Technology, Kiswahili, Social Studies, Creative Arts and Sports, Agriculture, and Religious Education.
