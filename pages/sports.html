Sports & Physical Education

Through sports, students develop physical fitness, essential life skills, social connections, and values that contribute to their personal growth and success in various aspects of life, including academics and future careers.

We offer a wide range of sports including football, basketball, volleyball, athletics, swimming, handball, and netball. Our qualified PE teachers and coaches ensure every student is engaged and challenged.
