About Kathaayoni Academy Junior School

Kathaayoni Academy Junior School is a mixed day and boarding school offering Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC) education. The school provides quality Grades 7–9 education, developing critical thinkers, innovators, and compassionate young citizens.

Located in Nairobi, Kenya, Kathaayoni Academy is easily accessible and provides a safe, nurturing, and stimulating environment for learners. Our staff are qualified, experienced, and passionate about education.

Our mission is to offer world-class junior school education grounded in Kenyan values, enabling every learner to discover and develop their unique God-given potential.
